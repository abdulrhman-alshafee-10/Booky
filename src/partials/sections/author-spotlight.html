Meet Eleanor Finch

Eleanor Finch writes luminous, character-driven fiction that lingers long after the final page. A two-time national book award finalist, her work explores memory, belonging, and the quiet courage of ordinary lives.

"I write for the reader who finishes a book and sits quietly for a moment, changed."
