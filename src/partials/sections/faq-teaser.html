eBooks are available immediately after your payment is confirmed — usually within seconds. You'll find your purchase in your Booky account under "My Library" and can start reading in your browser or download to the Booky app on any device.

All eBooks on Booky are DRM-free and available in EPUB and PDF formats. EPUB is compatible with most e-readers including Kindle (via email), Kobo, and Apple Books. PDF is ideal for formatted texts like graphic novels or academic titles.

Print books can be returned within 30 days in original condition for a full refund. For eBooks, we offer a 7-day satisfaction guarantee — if you've read less than 10% of the book, you can request a refund from your account dashboard.

Booky offers three tiers: Basic (free), Plus ($6/month), and Premium ($14/month). All paid plans include a 30-day free trial and can be cancelled at any time — no lock-in. Members save an average of 40% on every purchase compared to cover price.
