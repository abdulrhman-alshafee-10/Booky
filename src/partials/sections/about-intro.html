Our story

Born from a love of reading

Booky started in a tiny Edinburgh flat in 2012, when three friends who couldn't stop talking about books decided to build the bookstore they always wished existed — one that combined the charm of an independent shop with the convenience of the digital age.

Today, we're a team of 48 passionate readers, editors, and engineers helping over a million book lovers discover their next favourite read. We still hand-pick every title in our featured collections, just as we did on day one.
