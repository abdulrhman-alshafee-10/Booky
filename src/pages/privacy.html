1 · Overview

This policy explains how Booky handles your personal data. We collect only what we need to take your order, run your account, and improve the shop. This is a demonstration template — replace this copy with your own policy, reviewed by a qualified adviser, before going live.

2 · What we collect

Depending on how you use the site, we may hold:

  • Contact details you give us — name, email, delivery and billing addresses, phone number.
  • Order history and wishlist items, so you can review and reorder.
  • Technical data such as device type and pages visited, gathered through cookies.

We never store full payment card numbers; payments are handled by our payment provider.

3 · How we use it

We use your data to fulfil orders, provide customer support, keep your account secure, and — only with your consent — send you our newsletter. You can unsubscribe at any time in one click.

4 · Cookies

Essential cookies keep your cart and session working. Optional analytics cookies help us understand what readers enjoy. You can manage non-essential cookies through your browser settings at any time.

5 · Sharing

We share data only with the partners who help us run the shop — delivery carriers, our payment provider, and email tooling — and only what they need. We never sell your data.

6 · Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to stop using it for marketing. Most of this you can do yourself from your account settings; for anything else, just ask.

7 · Contact

Questions about your privacy? Write to privacy@booky.example.com or use our contact page.
