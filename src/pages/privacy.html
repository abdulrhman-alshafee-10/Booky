This Privacy Policy explains how Booky collects, uses, and protects information about you when you use our website or services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Data controller

The data controller is Booky Ltd, 14 Reading Lane, Edinburgh, EH1 1AA, United Kingdom. You can contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@booky.example.com.

2. Data we collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data: Name, email address, password (hashed), and account preferences.
  • Order data: Billing and delivery addresses, order history, payment method details (we store only the last four digits; full card data is processed by our payment provider).
  • Usage data: Pages viewed, search queries, wishlist contents, and click patterns — collected via cookies and server logs.
  • Communications: Content of messages you send to our support team.
  • Marketing preferences: Whether you have opted in to receive marketing emails.

3. How we use your data

We use your personal data to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders
  • Manage your account and membership
  • Send transactional emails (order confirmations, delivery updates)
  • Send marketing emails, if you have opted in
  • Personalise your browsing experience and recommendations
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Improve our website and services through analytics

4. Legal basis for processing

We process your data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance — for order fulfilment, account management, and customer support.
  • Legitimate interests — for fraud prevention, analytics, and service improvement.
  • Consent — for marketing emails and non-essential cookies. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligation — for compliance with tax and financial regulations.

5. Data sharing

We share your data only with:

  • Delivery carriers — to fulfil your orders (name and delivery address).
  • Payment processors — to process payments securely.
  • Email service providers — to send transactional and marketing emails.
  • Analytics providers — under data processing agreements, with data pseudonymised where possible.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for targeted advertising on third-party platforms without your consent.

6. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Order data is retained for 7 years to comply with financial regulations. You may request deletion of your account at any time — see section 7.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: Ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Restriction: Ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@booky.example.com. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk).

8. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for core functionality (shopping cart, login session). Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the site. Require consent.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your theme and language settings.

You can manage cookie preferences via the cookie banner on your first visit, or by adjusting your browser settings.

9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including TLS encryption in transit, hashed passwords, and strict access controls. While we take all reasonable precautions, no internet transmission is fully secure. Please use a strong, unique password for your Booky account.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or a prominent notice on the site. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

11. Contact us

For any privacy-related questions, contact our DPO at privacy@booky.example.com or write to us at Booky Ltd, 14 Reading Lane, Edinburgh, EH1 1AA.
