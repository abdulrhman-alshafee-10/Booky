Our story

A bookshop built for slow readers

We started with one shop, one window, and a stubborn belief that the right book finds you at exactly the right moment. Twenty years on, that's still the whole job.

Since 2006

From one window to a worldwide shelf

Booky opened on a rainy Tuesday with three shelves, a kettle, and a hand-lettered sign. The kettle is long gone; the habit of recommending books over a warm drink never left.

Today we ship hand-picked titles to readers in fourteen countries, host author evenings most weeks, and still write every shelf-talker by hand. Scale changed the logistics — never the taste.

What we stand for

Three things we refuse to rush

Curation over volume

Every title earns its place on the shelf. We'd rather hand-pick a thousand books we love than warehouse a million we don't.

Patience as a craft

Plain paper, recycled packaging, carbon-balanced delivery. Good things travel well when they're not in a hurry.

Readers first, always

No dark patterns, no upsell traps. A recommendation you can trust is the only growth strategy we've ever needed.
