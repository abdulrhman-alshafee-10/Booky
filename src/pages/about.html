We believe in the power of books.

Booky was founded in 2015 by a small group of avid readers who were tired of soulless recommendation algorithms. We set out to build something different — a bookstore run by people who actually read.

What we stand for

Our values

Reader First

Every decision we make is grounded in what's best for the reader — from curation to packaging to after-sales care.

Curated, Not Algorithmic

Our recommendations come from human editors who read, discuss, and argue about books — not from what sold most last Tuesday.

Community-Driven

Reading is better together. We invest in the communities, clubs, and conversations that form around books.

Sustainably Operated

Carbon-neutral shipping, recycled packaging, and a commitment to donating 1% of revenue to literacy programmes globally.

Independently Owned

We have no VC backers, no corporate parent. Our independence means we serve readers — not shareholders.

Trusted & Transparent

Clear pricing, honest reviews, no dark patterns. We earn trust one book at a time.

Since 2015

Our story

Ten years of building something we'd want to exist. Here's how we got here.

  1. 2015

    Founded in a shared flat

    Three friends and a shared hatred of bad book recommendations started Booky out of a kitchen in Edinburgh.

  2. 2017

    First 10,000 customers

    Word of mouth took us to 10,000 readers without a single paid ad. Our curation model was working.

  3. 2019

    Launched the Reading Club

    Monthly picks, member discounts, and community discussions. 50,000 members in the first year.

  4. 2022

    Expanded to audiobooks & eBooks

    Digital reading, same human curation. 100,000+ titles across all formats.

  5. Today

    500,000 readers & counting

    Still independently owned. Still run by readers. Still arguing about which books deserve five stars.
