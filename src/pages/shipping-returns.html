1. UK delivery

We offer the following delivery options for UK orders:

UK delivery options
Service Estimated time Cost
Standard 3–5 business days Free on orders over $35, otherwise $3.99
Express 1–2 business days $7.99
Next day (order by 2 PM) Next business day $9.99

Reading Club members receive free standard delivery on all UK orders regardless of order value.

Orders placed before 2 PM on a business day are dispatched the same day. Orders placed after 2 PM or on weekends are dispatched the next business day.

2. International delivery

We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International delivery costs and estimated times are calculated at checkout based on your delivery address. As a guide:

International delivery (approximate)
Region Estimated time From
Western Europe 5–8 business days $8.99
North America 7–12 business days $12.99
Australia & New Zealand 10–16 business days $14.99
Rest of world 12–21 business days $16.99

International customers are responsible for any import duties or taxes levied in their country. These are not included in our prices.

3. Order tracking

Once your order has been dispatched, you'll receive an email with a tracking link. You can also check the status of any order from the Orders section of your account at any time.

4. Missing & damaged orders

If your order hasn't arrived within the estimated delivery window, please first check your tracking link and any delivery notifications from the carrier. If the issue persists, contact us and we'll investigate immediately.

If your order arrives damaged, please take photos and contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We'll arrange a replacement or refund promptly.

5. Returns policy

We accept free returns on physical items within 30 days of delivery, provided the item is:

  • In its original condition (unread, unmarked, and with original packaging)
  • Not a personalised or custom item
  • Not a digital product (see section 8)

Items that are damaged, marked, or missing packaging may be refused or subject to a partial refund at our discretion.

6. How to return an item

  1. Go to My Orders in your account and select the order.
  2. Click "Return item" and select the reason for return.
  3. We'll email you a prepaid returns label within 24 hours.
  4. Pack the item securely and drop it off at any Royal Mail post box or post office.

We recommend obtaining proof of postage. We cannot be held responsible for items lost in transit on return.

7. Refunds

Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 3–5 business days of us receiving your return. You'll receive an email confirmation once the refund has been processed. Depending on your bank, it may take a further 3–5 working days to appear in your account.

8. Digital products

eBooks and audiobooks are delivered digitally and are available in your account immediately after purchase. Digital products cannot be returned once downloaded. If you experience a technical issue with a digital purchase, please contact us and we'll resolve it.

9. Contact us

If you have any questions about shipping or returns, please visit our FAQ page or contact us directly. We're available Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM.
