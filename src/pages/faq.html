Orders & Shipping

Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available at checkout. Same-day dispatch on orders placed before 2 PM on weekdays. International delivery times vary — see our shipping page.

Free standard delivery on all orders over $35. Orders under $35 are charged at a flat rate of $3.99. Reading Club members get free delivery on every order regardless of amount.

Yes — we ship to over 50 countries. See our shipping page for current rates and estimated arrival times by region.

Returns & Refunds

Yes — free returns within 30 days on any physical book in new, unopened condition. Start a return from your account orders page. Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days.

Digital products (eBooks and audiobooks) cannot be returned once downloaded, as per our digital content policy. If you experience a technical issue, please contact us and we'll help.

Account & Membership

The Booky Reading Club is a monthly membership. Members receive curated picks, 10% off all purchases, early access to new releases, free delivery on every order, and access to our exclusive online reading community. See full details on our membership page.

You can cancel anytime from your account settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep all member benefits until then.

eBooks & Digital

After purchase, eBooks appear instantly in your account under Downloads. They're compatible with any EPUB reader app on any device — including Kindle, Kobo, Apple Books, and Google Play Books.

Most titles on Booky are DRM-free, meaning you own the file and can read it on any device without restrictions. A small number of publisher agreements require DRM — these are labelled on the product page.
