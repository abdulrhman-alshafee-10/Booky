Phase 1 · Tokens

Typography

Prata — a high-contrast display serif — carries every heading at a single natural weight. Jost — a light geometric sans — handles body, UI and the letterspaced small-caps overlines. Hierarchy comes from size and case, never boldness.

Phase 1 · Tokens

Motion

Slow, soft, never bouncy. Two easings and four durations cover the whole template; GSAP choreography arrives in Phase 16 and always respects prefers-reduced-motion.

ease-out — cubic-bezier(.22, 1, .36, 1) · entrances, reveals

ease-smooth — cubic-bezier(.45, 0, .55, 1) · color & hover

fast 150ms — hovers, focus

base 300ms — drawers, fades

slow 600ms — scroll reveals

slower 900ms — hero entrance

Phase 6 · Sections

Heroes & commerce sections

Hero archetypes H1–H3 and the commerce section set (S1–S6, S27, S28), each a standalone partial that real demo pages compose from Phase 8. Sliders run on Swiper (drag · arrows · dots); the deal timer is countdown.js. Every product card keeps its four actions.

Phase 7 · Sections

Content sections & forms

The content section set (S7–S21, S29): newsletter, articles, app band, testimonials, stats, author spotlight, brand strip, CTA, FAQ, how-it-works, social grid, events, genre spotlight, cover wall, membership, and the trust strip. The newsletter and the demo contact form below validate with accessible inline errors via forms.js.
