About

Eleanor Finch was born in Cornwall and studied English Literature at Oxford. Her debut novel The Lantern of Aldridge Bay was longlisted for the Booker Prize, shortlisted for the Costa First Novel Award, and won the Sunday Times Best New Fiction prize.

She lives with her two cats and an unreasonable number of unread books.
