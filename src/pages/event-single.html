An Evening with Clara Wells: Writing at the Edge of Memory

  • Wednesday, 15 July 2026
  • 7:00 PM – 9:00 PM (doors 6:30 PM)
  • Booky Edinburgh, 14 Reading Lane, EH1 1AA

Join us for a rare in-person evening with Clara Wells, the bestselling author of The Cartographer's Daughter and Letters from a Silent House. Clara will be reading from her long-awaited third novel, Writing at the Edge of Memory, and in conversation with Booky editor Marta Valero.

The evening will include a 30-minute reading followed by a moderated discussion and open Q&A with the audience. There will be a book signing afterwards — copies available to purchase on the night.

Seating is limited to 80 guests. Booking is free but required. Doors open at 6:30 PM. We ask that guests are seated by 6:55 PM.
